Deep-Dive: Customer Segmentation (RFM)

Recency · Frequency · Monetary — quartile-scored, rule-based segments

Each of ApexPlanet's 947 customers is scored 1–4 on how recently they bought, how often, and how much they spent — then assigned to one of six business-rule segments below. This replaces month-over-month cohort retention (not viable here, since 94.5% of customers in this 12-month window ordered only once) with a segmentation view that is directly actionable for targeting.
